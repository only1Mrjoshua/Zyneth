Our Mission

To empower African shoppers with intelligent price tracking tools that help them make smarter purchasing decisions, save money, and get the best value from their online shopping experiences.

Nova is an intelligent price tracking platform designed specifically for African online shoppers. We monitor prices across all major e-commerce platforms and notify you when prices drop, helping you save money on every purchase.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about how Nova works

How often does Nova check prices?

Our system checks prices every 5 minutes for active trackers. For products with high price volatility or during sales events, we monitor even more frequently.

Is there a limit to how many products I can track?

Free users can track up to 5 products simultaneously. Premium plans offer unlimited tracking, advanced analytics, and priority support.

How accurate are the price predictions?

Our AI algorithms analyze 6 months of price history, seasonal trends, and market patterns to provide accurate predictions with over 85% accuracy rate.

Can I track products from any country?

Currently, we support platforms in Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, South Africa, and the United States. We're constantly adding support for more regions and platforms.

How quickly will I get alerts when prices drop?

You'll receive alerts within 5 minutes of a price drop. For critical price changes, our system sends immediate notifications.
